人民币升值对中国进出口贸易的影响

The impact of RMB appreciation on
China's import and export trade
摘要：
汇率问题是和国民经济密切相关的一个重要国际金融问题，汇率升值或贬值都会对一国的对外贸易产生重大影响。因此，探究人民币升值对中国进出口贸易的影响以及其对策是很有必要的。本论文首先介绍了人民币升值的背景和原因，其中包括经济学依据以及国际政治、经济因素的影响。第二部分阐述了论文中涉及到的关于汇率的理论和人民币汇率的现状，从正反两方面分析了人民币升值对中国进出口贸易的影响。第三部分就国家和企业层面提出了应该采取的措施及建议。最后做出总结，如果人民币升值太快，这对于中国进出口贸易来讲是极其不利的，我国政府和企业应该联合起来共同努力消除不利的影响。
Abstract:
The exchange rate is a vital international financial issue, which is closely related to national economy. The appreciation or depreciation of the exchange rate will have an important effect on foreign trade of a country. Therefore, it's necessary to explore the impact on China's import and export trade and its countermeasures.
This paper firstly introduce the background and reasons of the RMB appreciation, including the theory basis of economy, the impact of international politics and economy.
In the second part, the theory and present situation of exchange rate which are involved in this paper are expounded. Besides, the impact of RMB appreciation on China's import and export trade from both positive and negative sides is analyzed.
The third part puts forward some measures and suggestions that should be taken at national and enterprise level.
At the end, the paper concludes that it will be extremely detrimental to the import and export trade of China, if the RMB appreciates rapidly. The government should unite with enterprises and work together to eliminate the negative effect.
